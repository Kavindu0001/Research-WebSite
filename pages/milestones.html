Project Milestones

Key assessments and deliverables throughout the project lifecycle

📌 Project Proposal

Project Proposal Presentation

Initial project proposal presentation to the panel of judges, providing a comprehensive overview of the proposed research, objectives, and methodology.

Date: April 3rd, 2024 | Marks Allocated: 5%
📊 Progress Update 1

Progress Presentation - 1

First progress presentation showcasing 50% completion of the proposed solution. Evaluation of research progress and implementation status by the panel of judges.

Date: May 25th, 2024 | Marks Allocated: 10%
📈 Progress Update 2

Progress Presentation - 2

Second progress presentation demonstrating 90% completion of the solution. Comprehensive evaluation of research outcomes and system implementation by the assessment panel.

Date: September 7th, 2024 | Marks Allocated: 18%
🏆 Final Assessment

Final Assessment & Viva Voce

Final evaluation of the completed product and comprehensive viva examination. Judges will assess the overall quality of research, implementation, and presentation of findings.

Date: October 30th, 2024 | Marks Allocated: 25%
📝 Documentation

Final Documentation & Report

Submission of comprehensive project documentation including final report, design specifications, implementation details, and recommendations for future work.

Date: October 30th, 2024 | Marks Allocated: 20%
💼 Additional Work

Portfolio & Presentation Materials

Submission of additional portfolio materials, including research outputs, publications, demonstrations, and supplementary materials showcasing the project's achievements.

Date: October 30th, 2024 | Marks Allocated: 22%
